What is The Black Lives Matter Movement

Black Lives Matter started as a decentralized social movement, not to be confused with BLM Global Network Foundation, or other specific organizations that also use the term. With a goal to build a society in which Black people can live with dignity and respect, how we choose to support the movement matters now more than ever.

Who started the BLM movement?

The Black lives matter movment was started by three women Alicia Garz, Patrisse Cullors, and Opal Tometi. They created a Black-centered political will and movement building project called Black Lives Matter.
